<p><ul><li>Continuous Flight Augers</li><li>Bucket Augers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auger drilling machines come in various types, with two common market types being Continuous Flight Augers and Bucket Augers. Continuous Flight Augers are designed with a helical blade that allows for continuous drilling, making them ideal for creating deep holes quickly. Bucket Augers, on the other hand, feature a bucket-shaped drilling tool that is used for digging and removing soil. Both types of auger drilling machines are commonly used in construction and mining industries for creating foundation piles, boreholes, and conducting soil tests.</p></p>

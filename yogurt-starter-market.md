<p><p>Yogurt starter markets can be categorized into three main types: liquid yogurt starter, frozen yogurt starter, and direct throw yogurt starter. Liquid yogurt starter is a ready-to-use option that can be easily mixed into milk to kickstart the fermentation process. Frozen yogurt starter is a convenient option that can be stored for longer periods before use. Direct throw yogurt starter is a powdered form that can be directly added to milk without any prior preparation. These different types cater to varying preferences and convenience levels of consumers.</p></p>

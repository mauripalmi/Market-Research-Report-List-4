<p><p>Metal complex dyes are divided into two main types based on their chemical composition: 1:1 metal-complexes and 1:2 metal-complexes. 1:1 metal-complexes contain one metal atom for every dye molecule, resulting in a one-to-one ratio. On the other hand, 1:2 metal-complexes have two metal atoms for every dye molecule. Each type of metal-complex dye offers unique properties and characteristics, allowing for a diverse range of applications in industries such as textiles, paints, and cosmetics.</p></p>

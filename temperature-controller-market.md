<p><p>Temperature controllers are used in various industries to control the temperature of processes or equipment. The market types include On/Off Control, Proportional Control, and PID Control. On/Off Control simply turns the heating or cooling element on or off based on a set point. Proportional Control adjusts the intensity of the heating or cooling based on the deviation from the set point. PID Control combines proportional, integral, and derivative control to provide precise and stable temperature control. These different types cater to different applications and industries with varying requirements.</p></p>

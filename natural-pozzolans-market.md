<p><p>Natural pozzolans refer to materials containing reactive silica and alumina components that can react with calcium hydroxide to form cementitious compounds. Raw pozzolans are unprocessed materials, while calcined pozzolans have been thermally treated to enhance their reactivity. The raw pozzolan market involves the supply and demand of unprocessed natural pozzolans, whereas the calcined pozzolan market focuses on processed materials that offer improved performance in cementitious applications. Both markets cater to the construction industry's need for sustainable and high-performance supplementary cementitious materials.</p></p>

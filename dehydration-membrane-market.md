<p><p>The Dehydration Membrane Market comprises two main types: Gas Dehydration and Solvent Dehydration. Gas Dehydration involves removing water vapor from natural gas streams using membranes, while Solvent Dehydration utilizes a liquid solvent to absorb water vapor from gas streams. Gas Dehydration is typically used in applications where high purity gas is required, while Solvent Dehydration is more commonly used in industries such as petrochemical and natural gas processing. Both methods are essential for ensuring the efficiency and quality of gas processing operations.</p></p>

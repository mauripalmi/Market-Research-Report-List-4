Thermal Feedback equipment measures skin temperature changes. Neurofeedback equipment measures brainwave activity. Heart Rate Variability Feedback equipment measures the variation in time intervals between heartbeats. Electrodermal Activity Feedback equipment measures electrical conductance of the skin. Each type of equipment offers unique benefits for monitoring and managing various physiological responses, making them valuable tools for biofeedback practitioners and researchers.</p></p>

<p><p>Switchable glass is a type of glass that can change its transparency based on the application of an external stimulus. Electrochromic glass uses an electric current to control the tint of the glass. Thermochromic glass changes color in response to temperature fluctuations. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) glass consists of nanoparticles that align to control the amount of light passing through. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass uses liquid crystal droplets to adjust transparency. These different types of switchable glass offer versatility for various applications.</p></p>

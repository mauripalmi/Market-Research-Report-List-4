<p><p>Remote sensing technologies utilize different platforms to collect data from various environments. Airborne platforms involve using aircraft to capture imagery and data. Aquatic platforms employ vehicles like boats or underwater drones for underwater data collection. Space-based platforms use satellites to capture images and data from space. Terrestrial platforms utilize ground-based sensors for land-based data collection. Mobile terrestrial platforms involve using vehicles equipped with sensors for data collection on the move. Earthscope is a market that focuses on the collection of geophysical data on land using a network of instruments.</p></p>

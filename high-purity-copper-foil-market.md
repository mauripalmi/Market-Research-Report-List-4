<p><p>High Purity Copper Foil Market consists of two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced by rolling a piece of copper slab to achieve the desired thickness, making it more flexible and easier to manipulate. On the other hand, Electrolytic Copper Foil is made using an electrolysis process, resulting in a smoother and more uniform surface. Both types are widely used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction for their excellent conductivity and corrosion resistance.</p></p>

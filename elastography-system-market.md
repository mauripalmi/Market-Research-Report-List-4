<p><p>Elastography systems are used for non-invasive imaging of tissue stiffness, with three main types dominating the market: Ultrasound Elastography, Magnetic Resonance Elastography, and Others. Ultrasound Elastography utilizes sound waves to measure tissue stiffness, providing real-time imaging. Magnetic Resonance Elastography uses magnetic resonance imaging to create detailed maps of tissue stiffness, offering high accuracy. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies such as shear wave elastography and strain elastography, which offer different methods of measuring tissue stiffness.</p></p>

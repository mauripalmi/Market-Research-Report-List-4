<p><p>Steel forming services market includes various types such as Roll Forming and Extrusion Forming. Roll Forming involves continuous bending of a long strip of steel to achieve desired shapes, while Extrusion Forming utilizes a die to shape the steel by forcing it through the die opening. Both methods are popular in creating a variety of steel products with different shapes and sizes, catering to industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>

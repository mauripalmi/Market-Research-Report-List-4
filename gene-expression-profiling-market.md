<p><p>Gene Expression Profiling is a method used to measure the activity of genes in cells and tissues. RNA Sequencing analyzes the presence and quantity of RNA to determine gene expression levels. Real-Time Quantitative PCR is a technique that measures the amount of PCR product at each cycle of the reaction, providing a quantitative measurement of gene expression. Digital PCR is a more precise method of quantifying nucleic acids by dividing a sample into thousands of individual reactions, allowing for accurate gene expression analysis.</p></p>

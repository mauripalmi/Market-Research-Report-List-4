<p><strong>The Electrical Steel Coatings Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grain-oriented</li><li>Non-grain oriented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical steel coatings are used in both grain-oriented and non-grain oriented applications. Grain-oriented coatings are ideal for transformers and motors as they allow for efficient magnetic flux alignment. On the other hand, non-grain oriented coatings are well-suited for power generators and magnetic shields due to their isotropic properties. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and reliability of electrical steel components in various industrial applications.</p></p>

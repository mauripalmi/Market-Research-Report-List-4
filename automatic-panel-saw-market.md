<p><p>Automatic panel saws are classified into two types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic panel saws are equipped with advanced technology that allows for minimal human intervention, making them more efficient and productive. They are ideal for high-volume productions and require less skill to operate. On the other hand, semi-automatic panel saws have a combination of automated and manual features, offering more flexibility and control over the cutting process. They are suitable for small to medium-sized woodworking shops that require a balance between automation and human intervention.</p></p>

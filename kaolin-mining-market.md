<p><p>Kaolin mining market types include water-washed, airfloat, calcined, delaminated, surface-modified, and unprocessed kaolin. Water-washed kaolin is processed with water to remove impurities. Airfloat kaolin is a fine powder with high brightness. Calcined kaolin is heated to remove water and other volatile materials. Delaminated kaolin is separated into thin layers. Surface-modified kaolin has been chemically treated for specific properties. Unprocessed kaolin is raw material that has not undergone any processing. Each type serves different industrial applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>

<p><p>Basalt Continuous Fiber (BCF) market involves three main types - Chopped Fiber, Roving, and GLFT. Chopped Fiber is short lengths of fiber used in various composite applications. Roving is continuous strands of fiber twisted into a single strand, commonly used in textile industry and filament winding processes. GLFT, or Glass Like Fiber Tow, is a new type of basalt fiber that combines the properties of glass fibers with the advantages of basalt fibers, making it suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Direct marketing tools are categorized into various market types based on how they reach target customers. Direct mail involves sending physical promotional materials via mail. Telemarketing involves making sales calls to potential customers. Email marketing sends promotional content directly to potential customers' inboxes. Text (SMS) marketing sends promotional messages to customers' mobile devices. Handouts are physical materials distributed to customers in person. Social media marketing uses online platforms to reach customers. Direct selling involves selling products directly to customers without a middleman. Other direct marketing tools may include flyers, brochures, and catalogs.</p></p>

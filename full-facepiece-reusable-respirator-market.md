<p><p>Full facepiece reusable respirators in the market come in three main types based on their air supply mechanisms: self-contained air supply, gas cylinder supply, and dual air supply. Self-contained air supply respirators have a built-in air source, making them portable and ideal for remote or confined spaces. Gas cylinder supply respirators connect to a larger external air source, providing longer usage times. Dual air supply respirators can switch between a self-contained source and an external source, offering flexibility in various work environments.</p></p>

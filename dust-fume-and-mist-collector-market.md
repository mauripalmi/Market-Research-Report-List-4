<p><p>Dust, fume, and mist collector market consist of three main types: Media Filter Collectors, Centrifugal Collectors, and Electrostatic Collectors. Media Filter Collectors work by capturing particles on a filter surface. Centrifugal Collectors use centrifugal force to separate particles from the air stream. Electrostatic Collectors utilize high voltage charges to attract particles to collecting surfaces. Each type of collector has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different industries and applications in the air pollution control market.</p></p>

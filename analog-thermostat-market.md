<p><p>Analog thermostat market can be divided into two main types: Single Loop and Multiple Loops. Single Loop thermostats are relatively simpler and control one specific heating or cooling system. On the other hand, Multiple Loops thermostats are more complex and can control multiple heating or cooling systems independently. While Single Loop thermostats are more common in residential settings, Multiple Loops thermostats are typically used in commercial or industrial buildings where more precise temperature control is required for different zones or rooms.</p></p>

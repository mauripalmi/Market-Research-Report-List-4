<p><p>Glaucoma laser therapies are used to treat various types of glaucoma, including Chronic Open Angle Glaucoma, Acute Closed Angle Glaucoma, Secondary Glaucoma, and Normal-Tension Glaucoma. Chronic Open Angle Glaucoma is the most common type, characterized by gradual vision loss. Acute Closed Angle Glaucoma is a sudden increase in eye pressure leading to severe symptoms. Secondary Glaucoma is caused by other eye conditions or diseases. Normal-Tension Glaucoma is a type where damage to the optic nerve occurs despite normal eye pressure.</p></p>

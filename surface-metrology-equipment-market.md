<p><strong>The Surface Metrology Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface metrology equipment can be categorized into Contact and Non-Contact types based on the method of measurement they use. Contact metrology equipment uses physical contact with the surface being measured, such as a stylus or probe, to gather data. On the other hand, Non-Contact metrology equipment utilizes optical or laser technology to measure surface characteristics without touching the sample. Both types of equipment are essential for industries like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace for quality control and precision measurements.</p></p>

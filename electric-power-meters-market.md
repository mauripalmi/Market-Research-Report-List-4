There are different types of electric power meters based on the type of power they measure - active power meters measure the actual power consumed, reactive power meters measure the power that flows back and forth but doesn't do any work, and apparent power meters measure the combination of active and reactive power. Each type of power meter is essential for monitoring and managing energy usage effectively in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>

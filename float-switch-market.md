<p><p>The Float Switch market consists of three main types: Top-Mounted Type, Side-Mounted Type, and Special Type. Top-Mounted Type float switches are installed at the top of a tank or vessel and are mainly used for high-level detection. Side-Mounted Type float switches are installed on the side of a tank and are commonly used for both high and low-level detection. Special Type float switches are customized for specific applications or industries, offering unique features or functionalities tailored to meet specific requirements.</p></p>

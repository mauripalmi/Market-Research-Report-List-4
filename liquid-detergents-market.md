Weak Alkaline detergents have a slightly higher pH level than neutral detergents, making them more effective in removing tough stains and grease. On the other hand, Neutral detergents are gentle on clothes and skin due to their pH balance, making them suitable for delicate fabrics and sensitive skin. Both types are popular in the market for their specific cleaning purposes and benefits.</p></p>

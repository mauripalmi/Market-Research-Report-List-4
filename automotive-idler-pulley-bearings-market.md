<p><p>The Automotive Idler Pulley Bearings Market can be categorized into three main types: Pulley-Separated Type, Pulley-Integrated Type, and Toothed Type. Pulley-Separated Type features a separate pulley design that allows for easy replacement without affecting other components. Pulley-Integrated Type integrates the pulley directly into the bearing, reducing overall size and complexity. Toothed Type incorporates teeth on the pulley surface for improved belt grip and reduced slippage. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different automotive applications.</p></p>

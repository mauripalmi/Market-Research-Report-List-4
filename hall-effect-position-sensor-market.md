<p><p>Hall Effect Position Sensors are used in various applications to measure the position of an object. These sensors can be categorized into two main types based on their output signals: Analog Output and Digital Output. In Analog Output sensors, the output signal is a continuous voltage or current that varies linearly with the position of the object. On the other hand, Digital Output sensors provide a discrete signal representing different positions of the object. Both types of sensors are widely used in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen Implantable Ports</li><li>Double-Lumen Implantable Ports</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Implantable ports are classified into two main types: single-lumen and double-lumen. Single-lumen implantable ports have one channel for delivering medication or fluids, while double-lumen implantable ports have two separate channels for multiple functions such as infusion and blood collection. Single-lumen ports are commonly used for long-term medication administration, while double-lumen ports are utilized when simultaneous infusion and blood sampling are needed. Both types of implantable ports provide a reliable and convenient way for patients to receive treatment.</p></p>

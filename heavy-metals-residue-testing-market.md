Chromatography-based methods separate heavy metals in a sample for identification and quantification. Spectroscopy techniques analyze the interaction of metals with light. Immunoassays use antibodies to detect heavy metals. Other technologies like atomic absorption spectroscopy or inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry are also used for heavy metal testing, offering different advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Sensible Heat Storage</li><li>Latent Heat Storage</li><li>Thermochemical Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal energy storage market consists of three main types: Sensible Heat Storage, where heat is stored or released by changing the temperature of a material; Latent Heat Storage, which involves storing or releasing heat through a phase change; and Thermochemical Storage, which involves storing heat through chemical reactions. Each type offers unique benefits and applications, catering to different needs in various industries. Sensible heat storage is commonly used in domestic water heaters, while latent heat storage is used in solar power plants. Thermochemical storage has potential for advanced applications in the future.</p></p>

<p><p>Unvented hot water cylinders are available in two main types: direct heating and indirect heating. Direct heating cylinders have an electric immersion heater that directly heats the water inside the cylinder. Indirect heating cylinders, on the other hand, use a coil or heat exchanger to transfer heat from an external source, such as a boiler. Both types of cylinders are popular choices for providing hot water in homes and buildings, with each type offering its own benefits and advantages.</p></p>

<p><p>Aircraft line maintenance market includes two main types of services: transit checks and routine checks. Transit checks are quick inspections conducted between flights to ensure the aircraft's safety and operability, typically lasting around 30 minutes to 2 hours. Routine checks are more comprehensive inspections performed on a regular basis, such as daily or weekly, to maintain the aircraft's overall functionality and compliance with regulations. Both types of services are essential for keeping aircraft in optimal condition and ensuring safe and efficient operations.</p></p>

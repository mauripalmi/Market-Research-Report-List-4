<p><p>Teletherapy machines are advanced medical devices used in cancer treatment. The different types include Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT), which delivers precise radiation doses to tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Image-Guided Radiotherapy (IGRT) uses imaging technology to target tumors more accurately. Stereotactic technology delivers very focused radiation to small areas, often used for brain tumors. Proton Beam Therapy uses protons to target tumors with minimal damage to healthy tissues. These machines play a crucial role in improving cancer treatment outcomes.</p></p>

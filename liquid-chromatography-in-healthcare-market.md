<p><p>Liquid Chromatography in Healthcare Market types include Ion-Exchange Chromatography, which separates ions based on their charge, Partition Chromatography, which separates compounds based on their partitioning between two immiscible phases, Size-Exclusion Chromatography, which separates molecules based on their size, and Supercritical Fluid Chromatography, which uses supercritical fluids as the mobile phase. Each type of chromatography has its own unique applications and advantages in the healthcare industry, allowing for precise separation and analysis of various compounds and molecules.</p></p>

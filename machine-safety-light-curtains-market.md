<p><p>Machine safety light curtains are categorized into two main types: Type 2 and Type 4. Type 2 safety light curtains are designed for lower-risk applications where the primary hazard is accidental contact. They provide basic protection by immediately stopping the machine when the light curtain beam is broken. On the other hand, Type 4 safety light curtains are used in high-risk applications where serious injury or harm could occur. They offer more advanced features such as muting and blanking to ensure thorough protection for operators and equipment.</p></p>

<p><p>Disconnectors can be categorized into two main types: Centre-break Disconnectors and Double-break Disconnectors. Centre-break Disconnectors have a single point of disconnect in the center, making them more compact and easier to operate. On the other hand, Double-break Disconnectors have two points of disconnect, providing extra security and reliability. Both types are commonly used in electrical systems to isolate circuits for maintenance or repairs. The choice between the two depends on specific system requirements and preferences.</p></p>

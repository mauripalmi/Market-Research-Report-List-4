<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Stream Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Stream Gauge</li><li>Ultrasonic Stream Gauge</li><li>Turbine Stream Gauge</li><li>Magnetic Stream Gauge</li><li>Coriolis</li><li>Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stream gauge market consists of various types of devices for measuring flow rates in different applications. Positive Displacement stream gauges use a rotating mechanism to measure flow. Ultrasonic gauges use sound waves to determine flow. Turbine gauges utilize a spinning rotor. Magnetic gauges measure flow based on the conductivity of the fluid. Coriolis gauges measure flow by analyzing the twisting of the fluid. Vortex gauges measure flow by detecting vortices created by fluid flow. Each type has its advantages and is suited for different environments and industries.</p></p>

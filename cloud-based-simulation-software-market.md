<p><ul><li>Discrete Cloud-Based Simulation Software</li><li>Continuous Cloud-Based Simulation Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based simulation software market can be categorized into two main types: discrete and continuous. Discrete cloud-based simulation software is used to model systems where events occur at specific points in time, such as manufacturing processes or queuing systems. On the other hand, continuous cloud-based simulation software is used for systems where events occur continuously over time, such as fluid dynamics or weather forecasting. Both types of software offer businesses the ability to perform complex simulations without the need for expensive on-premise hardware.</p></p>

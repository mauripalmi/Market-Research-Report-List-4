<p><strong>The FACTS (Flexible AC Transmission System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Connected</li><li>Shunt</li><li>Series and Combined Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FACTS (Flexible AC Transmission System) Market Types consist of Shunt Connected, Shunt, Series, and Combined Series markets. Shunt Connected market involves devices connected in parallel with the transmission line to control voltage. Shunt market uses devices like SVC and STATCOM to regulate voltage. Series market includes devices like TCSC and SSSC connected in series with the transmission line to control impedance. Combined Series market combines shunt and series devices to provide both voltage and impedance control for improved power flow and stability in the grid.</p></p>

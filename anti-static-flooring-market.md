<p><p>Anti-static flooring is designed to reduce the risk of electrostatic discharge by grounding static electricity. There are two main types of anti-static flooring: Resilient and Non-Resilient. Resilient anti-static flooring includes materials like vinyl, rubber, or linoleum, which provide cushioning and flexibility. Non-resilient anti-static flooring includes materials like epoxy or conductive tiles, which are more rigid and durable. Both types of flooring are effective in preventing static electricity buildup and are commonly used in industries where electrostatic discharge can be damaging.</p></p>

There are two main types of endoscopes in the market - Rigid endoscopes and Flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes have a straight, inflexible tube and are used for procedures that require precise control and a stable view. Flexible endoscopes have a flexible tube that can bend and navigate through curved pathways, allowing for more thorough examinations of complex internal structures. Both types of endoscopes play a crucial role in medical diagnostics and treatments.</p></p>

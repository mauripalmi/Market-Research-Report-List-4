<p><p>Personnel Screening Inspection Systems utilize advanced technologies such as X-Ray Backscatter Imaging, Millimeter Wave Imaging, and Terahertz Imaging to detect concealed objects on individuals. X-Ray Backscatter Imaging technology uses low-dose X-rays to create images of objects hidden beneath clothing. Millimeter Wave Imaging technology emits radio waves to create a detailed image of an individual's body. Terahertz Imaging technology utilizes terahertz radiation to detect objects hidden on a person's body. These technologies play a crucial role in enhancing security measures at high-risk locations such as airports and government buildings.</p></p>

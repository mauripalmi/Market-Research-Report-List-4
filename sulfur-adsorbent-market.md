<p><p>Sulfur adsorbents are used to remove sulfur compounds from gases and liquids. The market for sulfur adsorbents is divided into three main types: solid, powder, and others. Solid adsorbents are typically in the form of pellets, beads, or granules and are commonly used in fixed bed adsorption systems. Powder adsorbents have a higher surface area and are used in fluidized bed systems. Other types of sulfur adsorbents include activated carbon, zeolites, and metal oxides, each with their own unique properties and applications in sulfur removal processes.</p></p>

<p><p>The recycling of waste batteries involves the collection and processing of used batteries to recover valuable materials such as lead, nickel, cadmium, and lithium. This circular approach helps to minimize waste and reduce the reliance on virgin resources, making the battery industry more sustainable in the long run. Overall, the recycling of waste batteries market is poised for significant growth in the coming years as more countries and industries prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility.</p></p>

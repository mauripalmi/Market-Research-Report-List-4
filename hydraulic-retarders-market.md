Single-stage retarders provide consistent braking power, dual-stage retarders offer two levels of braking force for different driving conditions, and multi-stage retarders provide multiple levels of braking power for enhanced control. Each type caters to specific performance requirements, offering varying levels of braking power and control for heavy vehicles operating in different environments.</p></p>

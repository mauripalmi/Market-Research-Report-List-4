<p><p>The Cast Polyurethane Prepolymer market is divided into two main types: Polyester Based and Polyether Based. Polyester Based prepolymers offer excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion, making them ideal for applications requiring high durability. Polyether Based prepolymers, on the other hand, exhibit superior flexibility and resistance to water, making them suitable for applications in moist or humid environments. Both types have unique properties that cater to specific needs in industries such as automotive, construction, and mining.</p></p>

<p><p>Chromosome microarray market consists of different types such as cDNA microarrays, Oligo DNA microarrays, BAC microarrays, and SNP microarrays. cDNA microarrays use complementary DNA sequences to detect gene expression levels. Oligo DNA microarrays utilize short synthetic DNA sequences for gene expression analysis. BAC microarrays use bacterial artificial chromosomes to study large DNA regions. SNP microarrays focus on single nucleotide polymorphisms for genotyping and detecting genetic variations. Each type offers unique advantages for studying gene expression and genetic variations.</p></p>

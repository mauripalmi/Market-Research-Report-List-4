<p><p>Industrial polarization cameras are categorized into fully polarized light and partially polarized light markets. Fully polarized light cameras capture images where the light waves are only vibrating in one direction, providing enhanced image contrast and reduced glare. Partially polarized light cameras capture images where the light waves are vibrating in multiple directions, offering a balance between image quality and flexibility. Both types of cameras are used in industrial applications for tasks such as material inspection, quality control, and defect detection.</p></p>

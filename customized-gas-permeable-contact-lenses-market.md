<p><p>Customized Gas Permeable Contact Lenses come in three main types: spherical, toric, and multifocal. Spherical lenses are used to correct nearsightedness or farsightedness. Toric lenses are designed to correct astigmatism by providing different powers in different meridians of the lens. Multifocal lenses have multiple powers in one lens to correct both near and distance vision. Each type of customized gas permeable contact lens is tailored to meet the specific needs of individual patients for clear and comfortable vision.</p></p>

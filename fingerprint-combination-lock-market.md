<p><p>Fingerprint combination locks come in various types such as Optical Scanner and Ultrasound locks. Optical Scanner locks use light and sensors to capture an image of the fingerprint. Ultrasound locks, on the other hand, use high-frequency sound waves to create a detailed image of the fingerprint. Both types offer advanced security features and are often used in high-security environments such as government facilities, corporate offices, and research labs. They provide quick and accurate access control by scanning and verifying unique fingerprint patterns.</p></p>

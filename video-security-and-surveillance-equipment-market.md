<p><p>Video Security and Surveillance Equipment market consists of two main types: IP Camera market and Analogue Camera market. IP cameras are digital cameras that transmit data over the internet, allowing for remote monitoring and recording. They offer higher resolution, advanced analytics, and easy integration with other devices. On the other hand, Analogue cameras are traditional CCTV cameras that require a physical connection to a recording device for monitoring. They are cost-effective but offer lower resolution and limited features compared to IP cameras. Both types serve different needs and cater to various market segments.</p></p>

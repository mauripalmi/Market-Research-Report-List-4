<p><p>The Optical Power and Energy Meter market can be divided into three main types: Pyroelectric, Photodiode, and Others. Pyroelectric meters work by measuring the changes in temperature caused by light absorption, making them suitable for high-speed and high-power applications. Photodiode meters utilize a semiconductor to convert light into electrical current, providing accurate and reliable measurements. The “Others” category includes alternative technologies such as thermopiles and calorimeters. Each type of meter has its unique strengths and is used in different applications based on their specific capabilities.</p></p>

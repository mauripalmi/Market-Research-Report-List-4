<p><p>Piezoelectric material surface acoustic wave filters are used in various applications such as IF SAW Filters and RF SAW Filters. IF SAW filters are used for intermediate frequency signal processing in communication devices, while RF SAW filters are used for radio frequency signal filtering in wireless communication systems. Both types of filters utilize the piezoelectric properties of the material to convert electrical signals into mechanical acoustic waves, allowing for high-frequency signal filtering with low insertion loss and high selectivity.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Wireless Tank Level Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Level Monitoring</li><li>Continuous Level Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless tank level monitoring systems are of two types: Point Level Monitoring and Continuous Level Monitoring. </p><p>Point Level Monitoring involves sensors that measure specific levels in a tank, such as high, low, or critical levels. This system is ideal for applications where monitoring discrete levels is sufficient.</p><p>Continuous Level Monitoring, on the other hand, provides real-time data on the exact level of liquid in a tank. This system is suitable for applications that require constant monitoring and precise measurement of tank levels.</p></p>

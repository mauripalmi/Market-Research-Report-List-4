<p><p>Thermoplastic storage tanks come in two main types: single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall tanks have a single layer of thermoplastic material, which may be more affordable but also less durable. Double wall tanks have an additional layer of protection, providing extra security against leaks and spills. Both types are commonly used for storing a variety of liquids, such as water, chemicals, and fuel. The choice between single wall and double wall tanks depends on the specific needs and requirements of the storage application.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Centralized Dispening</li><li>Distributed Dispening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated dispensing machines are divided into two main types: Centralized Dispensing and Distributed Dispensing. In centralized dispensing, all medications are stored and managed in a single central location, making it easier to track inventory and ensure accurate dispensing. Distributed dispensing, on the other hand, involves multiple smaller machines spread throughout a healthcare facility, allowing for easier access to medications in various departments. Both types aim to improve efficiency, accuracy, and safety in medication dispensing processes.</p></p>

<p><p>Exterior coatings for walls are available in various types, including solvent-thinned coatings for exterior walls and solvent-watered inorganic coatings for exterior walls. Solvent-thinned coatings are generally made with chemicals that evaporate quickly, making them easy to apply and providing a durable finish. On the other hand, solvent-watered inorganic coatings are made with a mixture of solvents and water, creating a long-lasting protective layer that is resistant to weathering and other external factors. Both types of coatings are popular choices for exterior wall protection and enhancement.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Powerboat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of market value for powerboat insurance: Actual Cash Value and Agreed Amount Value. Actual Cash Value takes into account depreciation and is based on the current market value of the boat at the time of the claim. Agreed Amount Value, on the other hand, is a predetermined value agreed upon by the insurer and the insured at the beginning of the policy. This amount is paid out in the event of a claim, regardless of depreciation.</p></p>

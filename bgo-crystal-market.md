<p><p>The BGO Crystal market offers two main types of crystals produced through different methods: Czochralski Method and Bridgman Method. Czochralski Method involves melting the materials in a crucible and slowly pulling a crystal seed from the molten material to form a crystal. Bridgman Method involves solidifying the material from a molten state in a gradient temperature zone. These methods offer different advantages and applications, catering to various needs in industries such as healthcare, defense, and research.</p></p>

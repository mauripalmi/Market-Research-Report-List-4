<p><p>Cement clinker is categorized into various types based on different characteristics and applications. Portland clinker is the most widely used type and is essential in the production of ordinary Portland cement. Sulfate resistant clinker is formulated to withstand exposure to sulfate-rich environments. Other types of clinker include low-heat, high alumina, and fast-setting clinkers, each designed for specific construction needs.</p></p>

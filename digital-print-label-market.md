<p><ul><li>Wet Glued Labels</li><li>Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels</li><li>Liner-less labels</li><li>Multi-part Barcode Labels</li><li>In-mold labels</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Label</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital print label market includes various types such as wet glued labels, pressure sensitive/self adhesive labels, liner-less labels, multi-part barcode labels, in-mold labels, and shrink sleeve labels. Wet glued labels are applied using adhesive, while pressure sensitive labels have a sticky backing. Liner-less labels do not have a backing paper, and multi-part barcode labels contain multiple layers. In-mold labels are fused with packaging during manufacturing, and shrink sleeve labels shrink to fit the product. Each type offers unique benefits for different packaging needs.</p></p>

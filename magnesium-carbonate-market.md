<p><p>Magnesium Carbonate market can be categorized into two types: Light Magnesium Carbonate and Heavy Magnesium Carbonate. Light Magnesium Carbonate is known for its high porosity and low density, making it ideal for applications in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food industries. On the other hand, Heavy Magnesium Carbonate is denser and has a lower surface area, making it suitable for industrial applications like rubber manufacturing and oil drilling. Both types of Magnesium Carbonate have distinct properties that cater to different markets and industries.</p></p>

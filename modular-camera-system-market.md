<p><p>Modular Camera System market includes two main types of camera modules, CMOS and CCD. CMOS modules utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing low power consumption and high sensitivity. On the other hand, CCD modules utilize charge-coupled device technology, offering superior image quality and high performance in low light conditions. Both types of modules are widely used in various applications such as smartphones, digital cameras, automotive cameras, and security cameras due to their unique features and capabilities.</p></p>

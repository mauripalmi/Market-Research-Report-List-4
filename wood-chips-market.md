<p><p>Wood chips market can be classified into two main types: Soft Wood Chips and Hard Wood Chips. Soft wood chips are produced mainly from coniferous trees such as pine and spruce, known for their faster growth rates and lower density. They are commonly used for paper and pulp production. On the other hand, hard wood chips are produced from hardwood trees like oak and maple, which have slower growth rates and higher density. They are often used for landscaping, fuel, and animal bedding.</p></p>

<p><p>UV Light Absorbers are chemical compounds used in various industries to protect materials from UV degradation. There are three main types of UV Light Absorbers: Benzotriazoles, Benzophenones, and Triazines. Benzotriazoles work by absorbing UV radiation and converting it into less harmful heat. Benzophenones act by absorbing UV light and dissipating it as heat. Triazines function by absorbing UV radiation and releasing it as longer-wavelength radiation. Each type of UV Light Absorber offers unique benefits and is used in different applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>

<p><p>Powder coating is a dry finishing process that involves applying a free-flowing, dry powder to a surface and then curing it with heat to create a tough, durable finish. This method is known for producing a high-quality, uniform coating that is resistant to chipping, scratching, fading, and other types of wear. Powder coating is commonly used in industries such as automotive, appliances, furniture, construction, and aerospace.</p><p>The Powder Coating Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.

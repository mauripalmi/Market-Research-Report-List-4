<p><p>The cardiovascular surgical devices market includes various types of devices such as perfusion disposables, cardiopulmonary bypass equipment, ventricular assist devices, cardiac ablation devices, and others. Perfusion disposables are used to maintain blood circulation during surgery, while cardiopulmonary bypass equipment supports heart and lung function. Ventricular assist devices help pump blood for patients with heart failure. Cardiac ablation devices are used to treat abnormal heart rhythms. The "Others" category includes additional devices and tools used in cardiovascular surgeries.</p></p>

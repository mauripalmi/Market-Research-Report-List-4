<p><p>The Automotive Semiconductor market comprises five main types of products: Processors, Analog ICs, Discrete Power components, Sensors, and Memory modules. Processors are responsible for the overall control and processing of data within the vehicle. Analog ICs enable the conversion of physical signals such as temperature or pressure into digital data. Discrete Power components regulate power distribution within the vehicle. Sensors detect and measure various parameters like speed or distance. Memory modules store and retrieve data for various vehicle functions. Each type plays a crucial role in the efficient functioning of automotive systems.</p></p>

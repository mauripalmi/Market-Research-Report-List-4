<p><p>Chemical dust suppressants are used to control dust on roads, construction sites, and industrial areas. The market types include lignin and asphalt emulsions, natural clays, and plant oils. Lignin and asphalt emulsions are effective in reducing dust by binding it to the road surface. Natural clays absorb moisture from the air, preventing dust particles from becoming airborne. Plant oils create a thin film on the surface, trapping dust particles. These suppressants offer different solutions for dust control in various industries.</p></p>

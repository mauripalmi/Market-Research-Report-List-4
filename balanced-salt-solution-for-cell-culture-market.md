<p><p>Balanced Salt Solutions (BSS) for cell culture come in various types, including Earle's Balanced Salt Solutions (EBSS), Dulbecco's Phosphate-buffered Saline (DPBS), and Hank's Balanced Salt Solutions (HBSS). EBSS is used for non-adherent cell lines, DPBS is commonly used for washing cells and diluting buffers, while HBSS is ideal for adherent cell lines. Other types of BSS on the market include specialized formulations tailored for specific cell culture applications, such as serum-free or custom media formulations. Each type of BSS is designed to provide the necessary nutrients and pH balance for optimal cell growth and viability in vitro.</p></p>

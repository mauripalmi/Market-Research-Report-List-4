<p><p>Lightning conductors, also known as lightning rods, come in various types including Franklin Lightning Conductor, Special Type Lightning Conductor, and Early Streamer Emission (ESE) Lightning Conductor. Franklin Lightning Conductors are the traditional type, consisting of a metal rod placed atop a building to attract lightning strikes. Special Type Lightning Conductors are customized to suit specific building structures. ESE Lightning Conductors have advanced technology that claims to emit a streamer earlier to intercept lightning strikes. Each type offers varying levels of protection against lightning damage, depending on the structure and location.</p></p>

<p><p>Personal cleaning product ingredients market types can be classified into three categories: Anionic cleaners, Non-ionic cleaners, and Amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners are known for their strong cleaning properties and ability to ionize in water, making them effective in removing dirt and oils. Non-ionic cleaners are gentle on surfaces and often used in laundry detergents. Amphoteric cleaners are versatile and suitable for a wide range of applications due to their ability to act as both anions and cations. Each type offers unique characteristics to meet different cleaning needs.</p></p>

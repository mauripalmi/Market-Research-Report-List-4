<p><p>Pallet strapping machines are divided into two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic pallet strapping machines require minimal human intervention as they are equipped with sensors and automatic tensioning mechanisms. On the other hand, semi-automatic pallet strapping machines require manual labor for feeding the strap and positioning the pallet. Full-automatic machines are commonly used in large-scale industrial settings where efficiency is crucial, while semi-automatic machines are more suitable for smaller operations that do not require continuous strapping.</p></p>

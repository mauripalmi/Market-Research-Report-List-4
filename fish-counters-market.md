<p><ul><li>Resistive Counter</li><li>Optical Counter</li><li>Hydroacoustic counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fish counters are devices used to monitor and track the number of fish passing through a specific area. There are three main types of fish counters on the market: Resistive counters function by detecting the change in electrical resistance caused by fish passing through, Optical counters work by counting the shadows created by fish passing through a beam of light, and Hydroacoustic counters use sound waves to create images of passing fish. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited to different environments and fish species.</p></p>

<p><p>Vehicle hydraulic brake fluid market is dominated by three main types: DOT3, DOT4, and DOT5. DOT3 is the most common type of brake fluid and is suitable for most vehicles. DOT4 has a higher boiling point than DOT3, making it ideal for vehicles with heavier loads or high-performance brakes. DOT5 is silicone-based and is often used in classic cars and motorcycles. Other types of brake fluids may include DOT5.1 or specialty fluids for specific vehicle applications. Each type has different characteristics and is used based on the vehicle's requirements.</p></p>

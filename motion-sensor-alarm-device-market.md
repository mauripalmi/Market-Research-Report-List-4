<p><p>Motion Sensor Alarm Devices come in various types such as Passive Infrared (PIR) Type, which detects body heat and movement, and Microwave (MW) Type, which emits microwave signals to detect motion. Other types include Ultrasonic and Tomographic types. PIR types are commonly used in residential settings while MW types are more suitable for outdoor applications due to their ability to penetrate through walls. Each type has its own set of advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Smart Hearing Aid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Behind-the-ear (BTE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-the-ear (ITE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-The-Canal (ITC) Hearing Aids</li><li>Completely-In-Canal (CIC) Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Hearing Aids come in various types to cater to different needs. Behind-the-ear (BTE) Hearing Aids are worn behind the ear and are suitable for all levels of hearing loss. In-the-ear (ITE) Hearing Aids are custom-made to fit inside the ear canal for a discreet option. In-The-Canal (ITC) Hearing Aids are smaller and fit partially inside the ear canal. Completely-In-Canal (CIC) Hearing Aids are the most discreet option as they sit completely inside the ear canal, nearly invisible to others.</p></p>

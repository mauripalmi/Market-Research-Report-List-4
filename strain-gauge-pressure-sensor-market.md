<p><p>Strain gauge pressure sensors are available in two main types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide continuous, real-time measurement data in the form of a voltage or current signal, making them ideal for applications requiring precise monitoring. Digital output sensors convert pressure readings into digital signals, which can be easily processed by microcontrollers or computers. These sensors are preferred for applications requiring data logging, automation, or integration with digital systems. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>

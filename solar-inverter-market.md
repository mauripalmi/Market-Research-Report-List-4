<p><ul><li>Central Solar Inverter</li><li>String Solar Inverter</li><li>Micro Solar Inverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central Solar Inverters are typically used in large-scale solar power plants, converting DC power from multiple solar panels into AC power for grid connection. String Solar Inverters are used in commercial and residential solar installations, converting DC power from a string of solar panels into AC power. Micro Solar Inverters are attached to individual solar panels, converting DC power at the panel level into AC power. Each type of inverter serves a specific market segment based on the scale and location of the solar installation.</p></p>

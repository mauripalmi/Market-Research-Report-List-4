<p><p>The adult powdered milk market consists of two main types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder is made by evaporating pasteurized milk to remove water, resulting in a creamy product with a higher fat content. Skim milk powder, on the other hand, is made by removing the fat from pasteurized milk before evaporating it, resulting in a lower-fat product. Both types are popular choices for adults looking for convenient and long-lasting milk alternatives.</p></p>

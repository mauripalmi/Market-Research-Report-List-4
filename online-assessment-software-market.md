<p><p>Online assessment software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on the provider's servers and accessed through a web browser, offering scalability and convenience. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and managed on the user's own servers, providing greater control and security. While cloud-based solutions are preferred for their flexibility and ease of use, on-premises solutions are often chosen by organizations with strict security requirements or limited internet access.</p></p>

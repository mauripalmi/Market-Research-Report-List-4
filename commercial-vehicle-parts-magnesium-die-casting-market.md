<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Gravity Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle parts made of magnesium using die casting technology come in various types such as Pressure Die Casting, Vacuum Die Casting, Squeeze Die Casting, and Gravity Die Casting. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten magnesium into a die under high pressure. Vacuum die casting involves removing air from the die cavity to reduce porosity. Squeeze die casting squeezes molten magnesium into the die cavity. Gravity die casting uses gravity to fill the die cavity with molten magnesium. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>

<p><p>Aquaponics Systems come in various types, with the most common being Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), Deep Water Culture (DWC), and Media Filled Grow Beds. NFT systems use a constant flow of nutrient-rich water to grow plants in a shallow stream. DWC systems involve suspending plant roots in nutrient-rich water. Media Filled Grow Beds use a growing medium like gravel or clay pebbles to support plant growth. Each market type offers unique advantages and can be tailored to specific needs and preferences.</p></p>

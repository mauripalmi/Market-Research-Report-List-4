<p><p>Core drills are commonly used in construction for drilling holes in materials such as concrete, brick, and stone. There are two types of core drills commonly used in the market: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills use a vacuum system to remove dust and debris while drilling, making them suitable for indoor use. Wet core drills use water to keep the drill bit cool and lubricated, making them ideal for larger, outdoor projects where dust control is not a priority.</p></p>

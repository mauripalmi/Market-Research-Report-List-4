<p><strong>The Fire Retardant Epoxy Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogenated Fire Retardant</li><li>Inorganic Fire Retardant</li><li>Organic Phosphorus Fire Retardant</li><li>Nitrogenous Fire Retardant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire retardant epoxy systems are categorized into four main types based on the type of fire retardant used: </p><p>1. Halogenated fire retardants use halogen elements like chlorine and bromine to inhibit combustion.</p><p>2. Inorganic fire retardants contain minerals such as alumina trihydrate which release water vapor when heated, suppressing flames.</p><p>3. Organic phosphorus fire retardants deprive the fire of oxygen by forming a char layer.</p><p>4. Nitrogenous fire retardants release nitrogen gas when exposed to high temperatures, diluting oxygen and inhibiting combustion. Each type offers unique benefits in fire protection applications.</p></p>

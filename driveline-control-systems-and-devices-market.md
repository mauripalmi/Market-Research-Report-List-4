<p><ul><li>Transfer Case</li><li>Fluid Coupling</li><li>Limited Slip Differential (LSD)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Driveline control systems and devices market includes various types such as transfer cases, fluid couplings, limited slip differentials (LSD), and others. Transfer cases are used in all-wheel drive and four-wheel drive vehicles to transfer power between the front and rear axles. Fluid couplings provide smooth power transmission without mechanical contact. Limited slip differentials (LSD) improve traction by distributing power between wheels based on slip. Other driveline control systems and devices include clutches, torque vectoring systems, and electronic stability control systems.</p></p>

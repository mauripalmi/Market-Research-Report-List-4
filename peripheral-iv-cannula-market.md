<p><p>The Peripheral IV Cannula market includes two main types: Y-shaped and straight. Y-shaped cannulas feature a design that allows for multiple infusion lines to be connected simultaneously, making them ideal for use in critically ill patients or in situations where multiple medications need to be administered. Straight cannulas, on the other hand, have a more simple design with a single infusion line, making them suitable for most routine IV procedures. Both types offer secure and reliable access for the administration of fluids and medications.</p></p>

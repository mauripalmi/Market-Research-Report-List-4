<p><strong>The Automotive Retread Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive retread tyre market consists of two main types: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. Pre Cure retreading involves applying a new tread rubber to a tire casing and curing it in a chamber press before being fitted to the vehicle. Mold Cure retreading, on the other hand, involves applying the tread rubber to the casing and curing it in a mold, resulting in a more uniform and precise tread pattern. Both methods provide cost-effective and environmentally friendly solutions for extending the life of tires.</p></p>

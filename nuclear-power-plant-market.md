<p><p>The Nuclear Power Plant market consists of different types of reactors such as Pressurized Water Reactor, Boiling Water Reactor, Heavy Water Reactor, and Fast Reactor. Pressurized Water Reactors use pressurized water to transfer heat and generate steam. Boiling Water Reactors directly produce steam in the reactor vessel. Heavy Water Reactors use heavy water as a moderator, while Fast Reactors use fast neutrons to sustain the nuclear reaction. Each type has unique features and advantages in terms of efficiency, safety, and waste management.</p></p>

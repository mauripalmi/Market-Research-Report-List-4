<p><p>Membrane technology for liquid and gas separations encompasses various types of processes such as Reverse Osmosis, Nanofiltration, Ultrafiltration, Microfiltration, and Electrochemical Processes. Reverse Osmosis involves forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities, while Nanofiltration focuses on separating ions and molecules. Ultrafiltration is used to separate larger particles, while Microfiltration removes even smaller particles. Electrochemical processes involve using an electric current to drive separation. These technologies are crucial for industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage production.</p></p>
